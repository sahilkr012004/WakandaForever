    About

    The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering aims to provide quality education to youngsters so that they can contribute to the development of the nation. Promoting Industry involvement in student projects, placement, joint R & D ventures, organizing collaborative programs with premier institutions are the prime objectives of the department.

    Head of Department :Dr. Surinder Singh

    Office phone: 01672-253118,01672-253117

    The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) was established in 1991. The Department started the Diploma program (02 years) in Electronics and Communication Engineering in the year 1991. Later in 1993, the Certificate program (02 years) in Servicing and Maintenance of Electronic Equipments (CSME) and Certificate program (02 years) in Maintenance of Television (CTV) were introduced. B. E. (03 years) in Electronics and Communication Engineering was started in the year 1993.
