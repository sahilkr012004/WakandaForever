    About

    The Central Library is housed in a modern building having all kinds of facilities for its best utilization by the student community. It has large number of volume of technical books along with a good collection of books on literature, general awareness, management and moral sciences etc. The central library is already subscribing to a large number of National and International journals. Besides print subscription users are having access to full text of articles from journals under e-Shodhsindhu Consortium. The central library is having all the modern facilities like Plasma TV; MMP; CTV; OHP; Slide Projector etc. A good collection of VCD’s as well as CD ROMs is also available in the library.

    Library Rules

    1. Only the authorized members of Library can make use of library.
    2. A person shall not engage in audible conversation in any part of the library to the annoyance of any other reader.
    3. Keep all you’re belonging like umbrella, handbags and personal books at the property counter.
    4. However you can take with you only 1-2 note books with you inside the library
    5. Show you identity card while you visit Library to the attendant at the entrance. Without I card you may be asked to leave the library.
    6. Get yourself checked at the Exit while you leave the library.
    7. A person shall not write upon, damage or mark any book belonging to the library.
    8. Please do not indulge in tearing off, marking or dog-eating of the pages of a book.
    9. While in library, maintain silence, Switch off your mobile phone during your stay in the library.
    10. Help in keeping your library neat and clean.
